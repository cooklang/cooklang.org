---
title: Classic Pasta Carbonara
servings: 4
time: 20 minutes
---

>>source: Traditional Italian Recipe
>>difficulty: medium

> This authentic carbonara uses only eggs, cheese, and guanciale.

= Ingredients Preparation =

Cut @guanciale{150%g}(or pancetta) into small cubes.
Grate @pecorino romano{100%g} and @parmigiano reggiano{50%g}.
Separate @eggs{4} -- use only the yolks for creamier sauce.

= Cooking =

Boil @spaghetti{400%g} in salted water for ~pasta{8%minutes}.
Meanwhile, cook guanciale in a #large pan{} until crispy.

[- Important: Remove pan from heat before adding eggs -]

Mix egg yolks with grated cheese and @black pepper{2%tsp}(freshly ground).
Drain pasta, reserving @pasta water{1%cup}.

== Final Steps ==

Add pasta to the #pan with guanciale.
Remove from heat and quickly mix in egg mixture.
Add pasta water gradually until creamy.
Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
