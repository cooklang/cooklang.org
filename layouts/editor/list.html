A focused workspace for your recipes

Built on a familiar VS Code-style editor, tuned for Cooklang

Write with confidence

Cooklang syntax highlighting, inline validation, and autocomplete for ingredients and cookware as you type.

Live recipe preview

See a formatted recipe alongside your text, with one-click scaling and tappable ingredient chips.

Shopping lists by aisle

Combine ingredients across multiple recipes into a single shopping list, grouped by store department.

Multi-day meal plans

Plan whole weeks with .menu files and compile their shopping lists in one step.

Meet CookBot, your AI sous-chef

A built-in AI sidebar that works directly on your recipe files — and always asks for your approval before it changes anything.

  • Import recipes from pasted text or a link, converted into clean Cooklang.
  • Draft meal plans from what you already have in your collection.
  • Edit across your library — tidy metadata, fix units, restructure steps — with every change shown for review first.

CookBot is the AI in Cook Editor, available with Cookbot Pro — €10/month, cancel anytime. Cook Editor itself is free and open source; everything above is part of Cookbot Pro.

Works with your phone, too

Cook Editor syncs with the iOS and Android apps, so the recipes you write at your desk are in your pocket at the shop.
