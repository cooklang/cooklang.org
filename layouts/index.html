Cooklang – Recipe Markup Language

Cooklang is a lightweight, open-source format for writing recipes as simple text files, meaning you can store, edit, share and sync your recipes with any service.

Get Started View on GitHub

How It Works

1

Write in Plain Text

2

Parse Anywhere

Use our CLI tools or mobile apps to render your recipes beautifully.

3

Sync & Share

Keep your recipes in Git, Dropbox, or any cloud service you prefer.

Simple & Powerful

Cooklang recipes are plain text files that you can use on any device, whether you're on your phone when cooking or on your computer when planning.

The flexibility makes it easy to back up and share your recipes, and Cooklang tools help process these files for various use cases. 

In a large #pot, bring @water{4%cups}
to a boil. Add @pasta{200%g} and 
cook for ~{10%minutes}.

Meanwhile, heat @olive oil{2%tbsp} in 
a #pan over medium heat. Add 
@garlic{2%cloves} and sauté until 
fragrant.
